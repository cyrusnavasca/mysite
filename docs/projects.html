2022 Diamond Price Analysis

For the following project, I implemented an in-depth regression analysis in order to examine what factors had an effect on diamond prices in 2022. I conducted feature selection, polynomial transformation and identification of unusual observations to obtain the final model. We ultimately found that carat, clarity and color had statistically significant effects on 2022 diamond prices.
